Why This Exists

A 30-Second Video Shouldn't Take Hours.

The Problem

Content creators juggle 3+ separate tools just to make one cohesive short-form video — hunting copyright-free footage, manually syncing cuts to a music beat, and fighting for a cinematic color grade without professional skills. Every tool switch breaks the flow and costs hours.

The Solution

A unified AI that handles the full short-form video stack in one flow. It sources matched copyright-free footage, locks visual cuts to the music beat automatically, and applies a cinematic color grade — no timeline expertise, no tool-switching, one professional output.
