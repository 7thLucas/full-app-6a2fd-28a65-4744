Foundation Validated

Current AI systems lack emotional reasoning

The Problem

AI systems today process information but cannot grasp the emotional dimensions of human experience—context, intent, subtle meaning, and the feelings behind decisions remain invisible to them.

The Solution

Today's AI excels at pattern recognition and data processing, but remains blind to the emotional context that drives human decision-making, communication, and connection. This creates a fundamental gap: machines that can't feel can't truly understand.

Target Audience

AI researchers, product teams building conversational systems, enterprises seeking more human-centered automation

Personal Motivation

Creating machines that don't just answer questions but understand what truly matters to people—their hopes, fears, and values.
